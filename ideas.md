# EVO Maritime – Design Ideas

## Approach 1: Deep-Sea Command Center
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Cyberpunk-Industrial Maritime
**Core Principles:** Dark command-room aesthetic; neon cyan data-stream accents; heavy typographic contrast between display and body; grid-circuit textures as background motifs
**Color Philosophy:** Near-black navy (#060d1a) as base — conveys depth, authority, and the ocean floor. Electric cyan (#00d4ff) as the single accent — references radar screens, sonar pings, and digital navigation. White text only, no grays.
**Layout Paradigm:** Full-bleed hero with diagonal clip-path section breaks; asymmetric two-column case study cards (image left, data right); horizontal scrolling stats ticker
**Signature Elements:** Animated glowing cyan underline on the EVO logo; circuit-board SVG pattern overlaid on section backgrounds; neon-bordered service cards with hover glow pulse
**Interaction Philosophy:** Scroll-triggered counter animations for stats; card hover lifts with cyan border glow; nav items underline from center outward
**Animation:** Framer Motion entrance animations — slide-up on section headings, stagger on service cards, count-up on numbers; subtle parallax on hero background
**Typography System:** Barlow Condensed (display/headings, all-caps, wide tracking) + Barlow (body, medium weight) — industrial, nautical, authoritative
</text>

## Approach 2: Maritime Intelligence Platform
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Precision-Tech / Dark SaaS
**Core Principles:** Data-forward layout; structured information hierarchy; restrained use of color — only cyan for critical emphasis; white space used aggressively within dark sections
**Color Philosophy:** Deep navy (#0a1628) base with subtle blue-gray (#0f2040) card surfaces. Cyan (#00c8e8) used only for numbers, CTAs, and key labels — making them pop like instrument readouts. Muted slate for secondary text.
**Layout Paradigm:** Sticky top nav with frosted glass blur; full-width sections with alternating dark/darker backgrounds; case studies in a masonry-like asymmetric grid; timeline as vertical spine with branching nodes
**Signature Elements:** Glowing stat counters with cyan ring halos; location cards with subtle map-pin icons and country flag accents; section dividers as thin cyan horizontal rules
**Interaction Philosophy:** Hover states reveal additional detail (case study cards expand); smooth anchor-scroll navigation; contact form with field-level validation glow
**Animation:** CSS keyframe pulse on service icons; Framer Motion stagger on card grids; scroll-triggered opacity fade-in for all sections
**Typography System:** Space Grotesk (headings — geometric, technical) + DM Sans (body — clean, readable) — modern tech-maritime feel without being aggressive
</text>

## Approach 3: Black Sea Atlas — CHOSEN
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Nautical Futurism / Editorial Dark
**Core Principles:** Dramatic full-bleed photography with dark overlays; editorial-scale typography that commands attention; cyan as a precision instrument — used sparingly for maximum impact; sections feel like chapters in a maritime atlas
**Color Philosophy:** Midnight navy (#070e1c) and charcoal (#0c1a2e) as the two background tones — alternating to create rhythm without using white. Cyan (#00d4ff) exclusively for accent lines, key numbers, and hover states. The palette references deep-sea navigation charts and radar displays.
**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric hero with large left-aligned headline and right-side stat callout; services in a 4×2 bordered grid with icon+text; case studies as horizontal split-screen cards (photo | data); network section as a stylized map with glowing location pins; five pillars as vertical columns with top-lit glow
**Signature Elements:** Thin cyan horizontal rule below the EVO logo wordmark; glowing neon-bordered cards that pulse on hover; large typographic numbers (50+, 4, 8) as decorative anchors in sections
**Interaction Philosophy:** Scroll reveals sections with slide-up + fade; service cards glow on hover with cyan border animation; mobile nav slides in from right as full-screen overlay
**Animation:** Framer Motion: hero text staggers in on load; section headings slide up on scroll; stat counters animate from 0 on viewport entry; card hover = translateY(-4px) + box-shadow glow
**Typography System:** Barlow Condensed ExtraBold (hero display, section titles — all caps, tight tracking, maximum impact) + Barlow Regular/Medium (body, card text) — the same typographic DNA as the presentation slides
</text>

## Selected Approach: **Black Sea Atlas — Nautical Futurism / Editorial Dark**

This approach mirrors the presentation's aesthetic most faithfully: dramatic dark backgrounds, electric cyan accents, bold condensed typography, and a sense of industrial precision. Every section will feel like a slide from the deck — but interactive and alive.
